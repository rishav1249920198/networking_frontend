🚀 Instant Signup Bonus:

Jab bhi koi naya student join karega, use turant 50 IC Welcome Bonus milega.
Agar wo kisi ke referral link se aaya hai, toh use extra points milenge. Isse registration conversion badhega.
📅 Daily Check-in System:

Student Dashboard ke top bar mein ek naya "✨ Daily Check-in" button hai.
Roz login karke click karne par student ko 10 IC reward milega.
🏆 Level-Up Commissions (Milestones):

Ab jitne zyada admissions student karwayega, uska commission rate utna hi upar jayega:
Standard: 3.0% (0-5 Adm)
Silver: 3.10% (6-10 Adm)
Gold: 3.20% (11-15 Adm)
Platinum: 3.35% (16+ Adm)
Student apne Earnings tab mein apna current "Tier" dekh sakta hai.
👤 Profile Completion Reward:

Sidebar mein ek naya "My Profile" tab hai.
Apni Details (Education, Address, Bio) pura karne par student ko 100 IC one-time bonus milega.
📊 Unified Earnings:

Dashboard Stats aur Leaderboard ab Automatic hai—isne Commissions aur sabhi Chote-mote Bonuses ko merge kar diya hai.
